# natural_evidence_v2 Claim Guardrails

Status: active for all v2 work.

## Allowed Current Claims

Current allowed claims are limited to planning and negative diagnosis:

- v1 completed a negative diagnostic under the tested passive/global-frame
  strict-token-index protocol;
- v2 is a planned controlled-natural micro-slot route;
- v2 has not yet shown payload recovery;
- v2 has not yet established FAR, robustness, or cross-family generality.

## Forbidden Claims

Do not claim:

- natural-output success;
- payload recovery;
- full FAR;
- stealth guarantee;
- robustness to paraphrase or sanitizers;
- cross-family generality;
- superiority over Scalable or Perinucleus;
- `24,000 fingerprints`;
- bucket-bank entries as fingerprints;
- old exact-slot or carrier-slot success as natural-output success.

## Wording Rules

Use:

```text
bucket-bank entries are natural next-token measurable opportunity/catalog entries
```

Do not use:

```text
bucket-bank entries are fingerprints
```

Use:

```text
v1 failed as a protocol under passive opportunity mining, global frames, and strict token-index anchors
```

Do not use:

```text
natural tokenizer evidence is impossible
```

Use:

```text
v2 is controlled-natural micro-slot evidence
```

Do not use:

```text
v2 is a structured evidence block
```

## Experiment Expansion Rules

Do not start:

- Qwen v2 E2E before teacher-forced gate passes;
- Llama before Qwen v2 positive recovery and null rejection;
- same-family null before Qwen v2 positive recovery and null rejection;
- sanitizer benchmark before Qwen and Llama positive recovery;
- paper rewrite with positive claims before Qwen v2 proof-of-life passes.

Any CPU/GPU work on Chimera must use Slurm. No CPU work should run directly on
the Chimera login node.
